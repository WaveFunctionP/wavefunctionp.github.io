Anybody got any Excedrin?

It's always fun to wake up in the morning, smell the fresh morning dew, and think about what new and exciting thing are going to happen today.

I didn't get to do that yesterday morning. I was too busy rushing to the power switch on my computer and ripping the case off to enjoy anything! Sometime early yesterday morning, the fans on my power supply went out and slowly roasted the internals of my PS. So what, big deal, replace the PS; you say. Well, replace the power supply I did.
