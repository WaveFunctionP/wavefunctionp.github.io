MIT Open Courseware

MIT has had a pilot program open for a while to their plan is to release all their course materials online.It's called OpenCourseWare. All the materials are free of charge and reflect the current curriculum being offered to students. They opened the program back in September, but at the moment the selection is pretty slim. Recently, one of my friends pointed me back in that direction again. This time I noticed some flash, shockwave, and java simulations offered in their physics department. Check out the 8.02-Electricity and Magnetism Course.
