The Stone Referrals

It seems that my site is a stopping point along the way to the Enigma! Numerous referals have been recorded in my server logs of people coming from this site. My best guess is that they are playing that internet game. The game is a mystery that you solve by using the internet to research with each mystery providing a clue to the "Enigma". What that is I don't know, but I did do one of the puzzles. Mine was on the Chupacabras. Of course, I didn't know that when I started though, but it was pretty easy to figure out.

The site was pretty freaky though. When I first got there I thought it was some kind of "new age" site. The site is still a little unnerving though, why I don't know. Kind of reminds me of that whole Blair Witch stuff a few years back.
