SETI To Go Active In 2003

SETI (Search for ExTraterestrial Intelligence) has announced plans to actively search for signals next year. SETI is a project to search for electromagnetic (and more recently laser/lightwave) communication from distant planets. It has compiled a list of the most promising sources Top 100 style. They plan to revisit those sites and complete an more extensive search. This is a departure from SETI's usually passive listening techniques whereby they just listen in whatever direction the radio telescope happens to be pointing. Maybe it'll prove more successful than to a previous project to relocate the source of that elusive WOW! signal received in 1977 and prompted a Senator to cut NASA funding for the SETI project.
