Bubble Chambers - Great Fun for the Whole Family!

Well, maybe not the whole family, but you can have fun. Kuro5hin has an article on creating a low budget bubble chamber. It looks rather easy to make, the only caveat is that you need to get some dry ice. If you can secure some, it's available in most cities, then your on your way to tracking your own cosmic rays and other delights. Hmm, yet another project to add to my every expanding list. The author also lists some other sources to get more information. I think this would make an excellent weekend project.
