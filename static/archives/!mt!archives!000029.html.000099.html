The U.S. Army has developed a tracking-targeting-firing system that can now knock a shell, moving faster than the speed of sound, right out of the air. Called Mobile Tactical High Energy Laser (MTHEL), it successfully destroyed a supersonic mortor round during test in White Sands, New Mexico. Check it Out!
