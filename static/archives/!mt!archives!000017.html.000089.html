Back and ready for action

Well, I got a new MB. Asus A7V333. This board is sweet. Instalation was nearly flawless. I'm not sure if that is a indication of good skills or a good board, but my Tyan installation didn't go nearly as well. I still dread sending the thing off to be repaired though. Not just because I got to pay for it to be shipped. But because the sooner I get my Tyan back, the sooner I have to give up the Asus. (It's supposed to be used in another computer I'm building for my parents.)

I took the SAT I today also. It was easier than I though it was going to be. I think I did well (not perfect though). I ran out of time on one of the math sections. The part that you have to fill in by hand. Oh well, time to prepare for the SAT II now. No time to be bummed out from missing a few questions.
