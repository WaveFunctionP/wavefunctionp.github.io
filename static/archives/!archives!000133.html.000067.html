Slow News

Hopefully I'm not the only one wondering why the news has been so slow lately. Nevertheless, despite the mediocre tempo as of late, I would be amiss if I didn't mention the recent announcement by Microsoft to pay out dividends. A first for the software giant and maybe a prelude of things to come from other technology companies, which have traditionally refrained from paying dividends. Instead, they have usually chosen to reinvest all earning back into R&D to further fuel growth. I suspect this move by MS is to change it's investor base to align it's value with more conservative investors and affirm its long term value. I might even go so far as to say that maybe this signals a more "mature" Microsoft. However knowing Microsoft, it's probably just more marketing than financial strategy.

Now if only I could find something more significant to report.
