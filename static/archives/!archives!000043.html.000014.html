November 19, 2002

AyuTrance album released.

Those of you who know me well, know that I am a huge fan of Ayumi Hamasaki. If you've never heard of her then you should, she only the most remixed artist of all time. I learned of the Japanese Diva during my stay in Japan and soon fell in love.
OK, I'll stop oogling, but not until I tell you about her new trance mix album. (You can get it at YesAsia.) Following in the sucess of the previous "CYBERTrance presents". AyuTrance 2 is a nonstop megamix, similar in style to those MEGA MIX albums so popular in Japan. I swear this stuff is like crack. And once you go JPop you never go back. Now all I got to do is survive through the torment and suspence as I wait for my copy. : (

(JTow remember to tell Art, I'm sure he's still frothing at the mouth from the last time I mention this coming. Well, now it's here. So now he can rest easy, if that's possible?!)
