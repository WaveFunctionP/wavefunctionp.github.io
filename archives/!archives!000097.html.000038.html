Geminid Meteor Shower

I seem to always catch these at the last moment. But if your reading this tonight, get your butt outside and check out the Geminid meteor shower . It'll be nowhere near as good as the Leonid showers, but it sould be worth your time. Provided your in the northern hemisphere, as I don't know how it'll be in Japan or Australia. I hate having missed the Leonids because of bad weather, but the weather is clear here now. So, hopefully, I'll be a very cold person coming morning. I guess I'll have to remember the coffee.
