Wired News: A Few Ways to Win Mortality War

Respirocytes are artificial red blood cells -- nano machines -- that Freitas sees as an emergency treatment that technicians could, for example, inject into a patient who has suffered smoke inhalation.

With an injection of just 5 cc's of respirocytes, he said, someone could run a 5-minute mile (12 mph) for 12 minutes without taking a breath.
