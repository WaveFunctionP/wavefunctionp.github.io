Genetic Research Rant

I've been waiting for news of this to comeout for some time now. Genomic research is so misunderstood and undiservingly feared.

The potential benefits simply outweigh the oportunity for abuse. Detractors often cite these type of projects as being similar to the atomic bomb. I don't see any one complaining about how low our oil prices are compared to other countries, or how low our electricity bills are. Nuclear reactors are a necessity now in our energy dependent society. And without prior research in to harnassing nuclear energy that was developed during the Manhattan Preject, we arguably wouldn't have these power plants.

The Bomb is a terrible use of technology, but I still don't see Iraq just up and dropping one on Saudi Arabia. Nor, do I see it happening anytime soon. The U.S. and the U.S.S.R. never got into open conflict simply because of it's tremendous power. Looks like a war deterrent to me. We have some crazy people in this world, but not many are that crazy. To get enough people together for a project of that kind of magnatude would require, MANY insane individuals. Hello, top that off with the fact that most mental instablities have an added side effect of some form isolation. So, now, how do you get a bunch of nutballs together. Go to a mental ward and you will see what I mean.

Also, many advocates say that such meddling in sacreligious and should be banned. I agree it is obviously in direct conflict with most religious views and consideration has been carefully taken to avoid claims of heresy, but science is a secular endevor. And, as such, is not confined by the conservative views of theology. The arguement that humans should not play God, is absolutley absurd in my opinion, because if we weren't supposed to; why then were we given this knowledge in the first place? And just why would we have a will to carry out such disputablely, reckless undertakings?

Conversely, Why not? To deny ourselves of our intelectual persuits, is to deny ourselves of the very thing which protects us from extiction.
