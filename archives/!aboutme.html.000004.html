This here is the obligatory, Bio page. This is where I tell you about myself and let you get to know the author. So with out further adu...

Most of you know me as "Der Feuhrer", but my real name is Micheal Thompson.
I'm aproximately 22 solar revolutions old.
I live in Petal, Mississippi. Which, by the way, is even smaller than it sounds.
Contrary to what my alias implies, I'm not German.
I'm an ex-Marine. MOS 4066.
I've been to Japan, Thailand, East Timor, and the Phillipines.
I love JPop.
I'm a huge fan of Ayumi Hamasaki. (Who isn't!)
I'm frequently absent-minded.
I'm also an MIT hopeful, I'll find out in the spring whether or not I get in.

Goals:

Graduate from MIT, or *eek* another university with a degree in Physics, and possibly Math too.
Earn a Ph.D.
Obtain a private pilot's liscense.
Learn to fly a helicopter.
Travel in Europe. I want to go to Germany, especially.
Learn German and (more) Japanese .
Get a job doing research full time.
Write a novel.

Why did I build this site?

I set out to make a page that's dedicated to the things I love, and enlighten a few strangers along the way.

I'm a "techno-evangelist". I love technology. It's the coolest thing in the world. And, I think that most people misunderstand it's function, and the way it affects the world. Also, I think most tech sites don't focus on thier mission, technology, and tend to drag people down into needless jargon. I try to cut through the hype and present a clear view of the world and with an emphasis on science and technology, especially through physics. Who knows whether I'll succeed or not, but I'm sure I'll have fun along the way. If it isn't; why do it?
