Evercrack

I've recently discovered Everquest. I know it's been out forever, but I've never played it or even seen someone who played it. Well, needless to say, I've developed an addiction. A very welcome one at that, as now I don't have to spend countless hours on the net scouring for some bit of needless news to satisfy my boredom. I don't expect I'll stop posting here, but the frequency will probably fade maybe about 2 or 3 times a week. So ever little halfway interesting news item won't be posted now. I'll only be posting the things I really have something to say about and that which is I find very interesting. Till then, I can be found on EQ, Vazelle, as Athenu or Aeriwen, my lovely level 5 Barbarian Figher and level 16 wood Elf Druid, respectively. Yes, it's totally nerdy, but definitely fun. Evercrack indeed!
