Gamespot Game of the Year Awards

Gamespot has released their Game of the Year Awards. I've only made it through the PC list, but I'd have to agree with them by most accounts. A great place to check out some of the best games this year has produced. Especially for those after christmas sales that are undoubtedly going on.

I can't recommend Warcraft III or Mafia enough as they provided many hours of excellent gameplay for me.
