Objective

I am open to offers for full time software developer positions. This should be a role where the primary responsibility is writing development/process tooling and/or production client software. I am most interested in employers that have an atmosphere conductive to professional development with senior developers who are available, performs code reviews regularly, utilizes version control and have implemented unit testing as a integral part of the work flow. I am not particularly interested in a dedicated quality assurance, developer in test, customer support, or technical sales positions.

About

I have a quiet, playful, introverted, but tactfully assertive personality. I highly value transparency and simplicity. And I know it may sound a little silly, but I love efficiency, quality, and process optimization. Much of my professional experience involves a fair amount of this work. I am often able to communicate complex ideas quickly, accurately, and clearly. I also enjoy teaching others and learning new things. I believe that stubborn resolve and rapid iteration are the most valuable skills for problem solving, not miracle methodologies.

I do prefer to work alone or loosely in a small team, but I am able to work effectively within a larger team given adequate autonomy and ownership. I also have some limited experience small team leadership, public speaking and community/customer interaction.

For leisure, I love playing and working on video games. In particular, I'm heavily involved in World of Warcraft as a community leader and a heroic progression rogue, and in Kerbal Space Program as a mod developer. I am also involved in my local Crossfit affiliate and I love to play golf when I can. I also read voraciously on the topics of computer science, sustainability, physics, and nutrition.
